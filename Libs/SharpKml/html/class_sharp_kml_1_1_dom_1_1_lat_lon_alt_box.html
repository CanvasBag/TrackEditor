◆ MaximumAltitude

double SharpKml.Dom.LatLonAltBox.MaximumAltitude
getset

Gets or sets the maximum altitude in meters above the vertical datum.

This property is affected by AltitudeMode and must be greater than or equal to MinimumAltitude. If both MinimumAltitude and MaximumAltitude are specified, AltitudeMode shall not have a value of Dom.AltitudeMode.ClampToGround.

◆ MinimumAltitude

double SharpKml.Dom.LatLonAltBox.MinimumAltitude
getset

Gets or sets the minimum altitude in meters above the vertical datum.

This property is affected by AltitudeMode and must be less than or equal to MaximumAltitude. If both MinimumAltitude and MaximumAltitude are specified, AltitudeMode shall not have a value of Dom.AltitudeMode.ClampToGround.
