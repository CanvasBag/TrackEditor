◆ Extrude

bool SharpKml.Dom.Polygon.Extrude
getset

Gets or sets whether to connect a geometry to the ground.

Only the outer boundary should be extruded. The geometry is extruded toward the Earth's center of mass. To extrude a geometry, the AltitudeMode shall be either Dom.AltitudeMode.RelativeToGround or Dom.AltitudeMode.Absolute, and the altitude component should be greater than 0 (that is, in the air).
