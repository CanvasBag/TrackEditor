Gets or sets the type of the field.

The type content shall be formatted according to one of the following XML Schema types:

  • xsd:boolean Compatible with System.Boolean Legal values are true, false, 1 (which indicates true), and 0 (which indicates false).
  • xsd:double Compatible with System.Double
  • xsd:int Compatible with System.Int32
  • xsd:float Compatible with System.Single
  • xsd:short Compatible with System.Int16
  • xsd:string Compatible with System.String
  • xsd:unsignedInt Compatible with System.UInt32
  • xsd:unsignedShort Compatible with System.UInt16
